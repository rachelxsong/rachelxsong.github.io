I am currently a Ph.D. student in social psychology at the University of Washington. I work in the Culture, Diversity, and Health Lab directed by Dr. Cynthia Levine.

Previously, I was a lab manager at Stanford SPARQ, a social psychology "do tank" that bridges science and practice to address racial disparities. I received my B.A. from Pomona College where I studied psychology and Asian American studies.
