How do institutions characterize race and racism?

Racism can be understood in different ways - as individual acts of discrimination or as systems of privilege. My research explores how companies and schools characterize racism and diversity and how these concepts influence public perceptions of the organization.

Presentations:

    The impact of George Floyd's death on how universities and companies talk about racism (SPSP 2021 poster)
    How diversity frames influence support for diversity initiatives: The role of psychological standing (SPSP 2019 slides)
